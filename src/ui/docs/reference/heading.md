# Heading

Polymorphic (`as` required).

## Props

Base: rendered element attributes, margin props, trim props, selectable props.

- `as`: Heading level. `'h1' | 'h2' | 'h3' | 'h4' | 'h5' | 'h6'`.
- `size` (=`6`): Visual size, independent of level. `1 | 2 | 3 | 4 | 5 | 6 | 7 | 8 | 9`.
- `weight` (=`'bold'`): Font weight. `'light' | 'regular' | 'medium' | 'bold'`.
- `align`: Text alignment. `'left' | 'center' | 'right'`.
- `color`: Text color emphasis. `'lowContrast' | 'highContrast'`.
